About Abia

A Mind Fascinated
by Other Minds

I am a psychology undergraduate at Delhi University — drawn to the intersection of research rigour and human compassion.

My academic journey is guided by curiosity about why people think, feel, and behave the way they do. Through internships at child-rights NGOs, research projects, and creative endeavours, I've developed a practice grounded in evidence and empathy.

I believe the most meaningful work in psychology lives at the boundary between science and storytelling — translating complex data into insight that genuinely helps people.

— Abia Ansari
